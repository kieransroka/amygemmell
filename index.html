ABOUT

I am a multidisciplinary artist based in Glasgow. My main goal as a creative is to bring accessible pieces of art into everyday life. A lot of my inspiration comes from nature, anecdotal experience, and domestic design.

As well as playful annotations to childhood nostalgia, my work often utilises colour, pattern, and abstract forms. An important element within my practice is being as sustainable as possible, using recycled and secondhand materials, as well as the majority of items being made by hand.
